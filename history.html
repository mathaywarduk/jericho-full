Inherited land, the understanding of how to grow and cook food and a warm welcome to our table.

A farm upbringing instils practicality and passion, a stubborn resolve and the creativity to use what we have got. A 200 year history farming Jericho inspired the restaurant and drives our passion for humble and honest hospitality.

Restaurant Jericho

Self taught, Rich and Grace have created a beautiful space using materials salvaged from Jericho Farm. Having learnt the industry over the last 7 years they opened Jericho in 2022.

Committed to individuality, they give a warm and honest welcome to every guest.
